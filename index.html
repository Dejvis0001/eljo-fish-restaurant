Welcome To Our Hotel

Find The Best Hotel In Sarande.


Discover a serene retreat in Sarande at Eljo Fish Restaurant, just an 8-minute walk from Sarande Beach 9 and near Paradise Beach. Our air-conditioned apartments feature private entrances, terraces with pool views, seating areas, satellite flat-screen TVs, fully equipped kitchens, and private bathrooms. Each unit also includes a balcony with outdoor dining and city views.


Enjoy our seasonal outdoor pool and kids' pool, perfect for relaxation and family fun. Sarande Beach 9 is an 11-minute walk away, and Butrint National Park is just 1.6 miles from our location. Conveniently, Corfu International Airport is 57 miles away.


Join us at Eljo Fish Restaurant for a comfortable and memorable stay in beautiful Sarande.
