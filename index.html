Eljo Fish Restaurant

Eljo Fish Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Sarande, Albania, offers an unforgettable dining experience with exclusive seafood dishes and a serene coastal ambiance. Enjoy the finest Mediterranean flavors in a welcoming atmosphere. Our menu features a variety of fresh seafood, skillfully prepared to delight your taste buds.

Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner, a family outing, or a special celebration, Eljo Fish Restaurant is the perfect destination for an exquisite dining experience.
